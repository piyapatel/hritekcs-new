The future of automation with TIA

The Siemens Electronics Works Amberg (EWA) lives and breathes the TIA automation strategy. It’s where the TIA portfolio can be said to produce itself: for example, the SIMATIC S7-1500 controllers that are manufactured there but also control the production machinery.

This is digitalization and automation in perfect harmony – with many benefits that led to EWA being named a Lighthouse Factory by the World Economic Forum (WEF) in 2021. In Amberg, Siemens manufactures more than 1,200 product versions and about 17 million SIMATIC products every year.
